Crusty Artisan Bread

This is a beginner friendly no-knead crusty artisan bread that is very easy to master. With only 4 ingredients, you are likely to have all you need already in your pantry. With this recipe you can have fresh bread in as little as 3 hours! This is an ideal side for home made pasta, impressive sandwiches or just a treat with your favorite spread or preserve.

Ingredients

  • 3.25 Cups Flour
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 2 Tsp Instant Yeast
  • 1.5 Cups Warm water

Steps

  1. Combine flour, salt and yeast in a medium mixing bowl
  2. Add Warm water until fully combined and texture is sticky
  3. Add more water if dough is too dry and more flour if dough is too wet
  4. Cover dough with a kitchen towel and let rise 2 hours
  5. Preheat oven with dutch oven inside to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  6. On a floured surface fold dough until a ball is formed
  7. Place dough on a sheet of parchment paper and score lightly with a knife
  8. Place the parchment paper and dough inside the hot dutch oven and bake for 40 minutes
  9. Once golden brown remove dutch oven and turn off the oven
  10. Let rest 10 minutes before removing bread
  11. Allow bread to cool completely before cutting
  12. Store bread in an airtight container at room temperature
