Garlic Hummus

Hummus is such a versatile spread. It goes well on crackers, bread, sandwiches, wraps and many other delicious foods. Once you learn to make this from scratch and see just how easy and affordable it is, you'll never buy it again!

Ingredients

  • 1 15oz Can of Chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 3 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  • Juice of 1 Lemon
  • 1/3 Cup of Tahini
  • 3/4 Tsp Sea Salt
  • 2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Tsp Ground Cumin
  • 2 Tbsp Cold Water
  • Optional Toppings: Extra Drizzle of Olive Oil, Fresh Parsley, Sprinkle of Paprika, Toasted Pine Nuts, Extra Garlic

Steps

  1. Add tahini, cold water, olive oil, cumin, salt, garlic and lemon juice to a food processor. Puree until smooth
  2. Add in the chickpeas. Puree for 3-4 minutes, pausing to scrape the sides of the food processor bowl, until smooth
  3. Add additional water if it seems too thick
  4. Taste and season with additional salt, cumin, garlic or lemon juice if desired
  5. Serve immediately, garnish with optional toppings
  6. Transfer remaining hummus to an airtight container and refrigerate up to 3 days
