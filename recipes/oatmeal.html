Brown Sugar Oatmeal

Oatmeal is sometimes considered a boring breakfast, but I firmly believe that is due to the association with instant oatmeal. Fresh stove-top oatmeal is rich, fluffy and indulgent. This is quickly going to become a breakfast staple in your home!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Steel-Cut Oats
  • 3 Cups of Milk of Choice
  • 3 Cups of Water
  • Dash of salt
  • 1/2 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup of Pure Maple Syrup

Instructions

  1. Combine oats, milk of choice, water and salt in a large saucepan
  2. Bring to a boil on stove top while stirring frequently
  3. Once it has started to boil reduce the head to low
  4. Add brown sugar and maple syrup
  5. Cook for an additional 4-6 minutes until thick, stirring frequently
  6. Serve immediately and store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator
